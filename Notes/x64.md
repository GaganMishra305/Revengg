# x64

## 1. Data segment:
1. byte: (db) 1byte
2. word: (dw) 2bytes
3. doublw word: (dd) 4bytes
4. quad word: (dq) 8bytes
5. xmm word: (xmmword) 16bytes
6. ymm word: (ymmword) 32bytes
7. real4: 4bytes floating point
8. real8: 8bytes floating point
